Notes

Three constituencies were suspended due to candidate death or court disputes. Of 42,779 polling stations nationwide, we could geolocate 39,761 (2,603 were not geolocated). Vote counts come from Election Commission publications, while polling station locations were sourced from a now-defunct EC website. Union boundary data comes from geoBoundaries under a CC-BY 4.0 license. Union linkage is polygon-based and may be inaccurate in rare cases; some unions may span multiple constituencies.
